About

I am writing this as I work it out.

I am Dylan Jones. I have spent the last few years at university, getting involved in societies, meeting people I would never have met otherwise, trying things that sounded slightly too big for me, and slowly learning that most good things start with putting your hand up.

Bertie is where I am putting the things I want to write about: university, work, friends, cities, books, awkward lessons, good decisions made late, and the occasional thought that survives longer than a notes app draft.

I like writing that feels direct. Not polished to death. Not pretending to have the whole answer. Just honest enough to be useful, and specific enough that it could only have come from one person.

At the moment I am finishing university, looking back at what it taught me, and trying to keep hold of the stories before they become vague. This is the place for that.
